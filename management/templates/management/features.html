All Maktab Software Features

Everything your Maktab, Madrasah or Islamic coaching center needs to run smoothly — from student enrollment to fee receipts to parent portal.

Student Management

Manage your entire maktab student lifecycle — from enrollment to graduation. Create detailed student profiles with photos, guardian information, contact details, and academic history.

  • Student enrollment & profiles
  • Bulk import via Excel
  • Assign to courses & batches
  • Photo upload & ID generation
  • Guardian & contact details
  • Search & filter students
  • Student result tracking
  • Admission application flow

Attendance Tracking

Batch-wise daily attendance for students and staff. Track presence, absence, and leave with monthly summary reports. The fastest way to mark attendance for your maktab.

  • Batch-wise student attendance
  • Staff attendance tracking
  • Monthly attendance reports
  • Leave management
  • Quick one-tap marking
  • Attendance percentage calculation

Fee Collection & Receipts

Collect fees from students, generate professional printed receipts, track pending payments, and get a complete overview of your maktab's fee collection status.

  • Record fee payments
  • Printable fee receipts
  • Pending fee tracking
  • Batch-wise fee reports
  • Payment history per student
  • Multi-currency support

Staff & Payroll Management

Manage your maktab's teachers and staff with detailed profiles, attendance tracking, leave management, salary components, and monthly payroll generation.

  • Staff profiles & roles
  • Salary components setup
  • Monthly payroll generation
  • Leave request & approval
  • Staff self-service portal
  • Auto-generated staff IDs

Parent Portal

Give parents their own login to track their child's progress. Parents can view attendance records, fee payment history, and academic results — all from their phone.

  • Dedicated parent login
  • View child's attendance
  • Fee payment status
  • Academic results
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Real-time updates

Courses & Batch Management

Organize your maktab into courses (Hifz, Nazira, Aalim, Deeniyat, etc.) and batches. Assign teachers, set timetables, and manage students batch-wise.

  • Create courses (Hifz, Nazira, etc.)
  • Organize into batches
  • Assign teachers to batches
  • Batch-wise timetable
  • Course fee configuration
  • Student batch transfers

Accounts & Expense Tracking

Keep your maktab's finances in order. Track all income from fees, record expenses by category, view accounts overview, and make informed financial decisions.

  • Income & expense tracking
  • Expense categories
  • Accounts overview dashboard
  • Financial reports
