Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Maktab management software

What is Maktab management software?
Maktab management software is a digital solution designed to help Islamic educational institutions (Maktabs, Madrasahs, Islamic coaching centers) manage their daily operations. It includes student enrollment, attendance tracking, fee collection, staff management, payroll, parent portal and more — all in one easy-to-use platform.
Is Maktab software free to use?
Yes! You can sign up and start using Maktab for free. No credit card is required. The software is designed to be accessible to all Islamic educational institutions, whether small or large.
Can parents access their child's information?
Yes! Maktab includes a dedicated Parent Portal where parents can log in to view their child's attendance, fee payment status, and academic results in real-time.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Absolutely! Maktab is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works perfectly on any smartphone, tablet, or computer. You can even install it on your home screen like a native app — no app store needed.
What features does the maktab software include?
Maktab includes: Student Management, Attendance Tracking (student & staff), Fee Collection with printed receipts, Course & Batch Management, Staff Profiles & Payroll, Parent Portal, Expense Tracking, Accounts Overview, Calendar & Timetable, and more. It's a complete ERP for your maktab or madrasah.
How is this different from other school management software?
Unlike generic school ERPs, Maktab is purpose-built for Islamic educational institutions. It understands the unique workflow of Maktabs and Madrasahs — batch-based teaching (Hifz, Nazira, Aalim courses), Islamic calendar integration, Urdu/Arabic language support, and fee structures common to Islamic coaching centers.
